Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

Short version: We collect your email only if you give it to us. We use it to send your downloads and notify you about new tools. We never sell it. Your photos never leave your device.

Who we are

Photo Organization System is operated by Maddie Galloway (maddie@elevatealign.com). This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you purchase or use our tools.

What data we collect

  • Email address — only if you choose to provide it. We collect this when you request your download links be emailed to you, or when you join the waitlist for upcoming tools.
  • Payment information — handled entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your card details.
  • Your photos and files — we collect nothing. The desktop sorting tool runs entirely in your browser and only accesses files on your own computer. Nothing is uploaded, stored, or transmitted anywhere.

How we use your email

  • To send you your purchase download links when requested
  • To notify you when new tools (like the Google Photos Sorter) are ready for early access
  • To respond to support questions you send us

We do not send marketing emails, newsletters, or promotional content without your explicit opt-in.

Third parties we use

  • Stripe — processes payments. Subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Google (Gmail / Apps Script) — used to send your delivery email from our Gmail account. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub Pages — hosts this website. Subject to GitHub's Privacy Policy.

We do not sell, rent, or share your email with any other third parties.

Your photos stay on your device

The Photo Sorter tool runs entirely inside your browser. When you use it, your photos are read directly from your computer or device — they are never uploaded to our servers, never stored in the cloud by us, and never seen by anyone other than you. The tool has no backend. Nothing leaves your machine.

When you use the Google Photos Sorter, your photos remain in your Google Photos account. We access them only to display them to you during sorting, using Google's official API with your permission. We do not store, copy, or retain any photos.

Data retention

If you provide your email for download delivery, we retain it only as long as needed to send the requested email. Waitlist emails are retained until the tool launches and waitlist notifications have been sent, after which they are deleted.

Your rights

You can ask us to delete your email from our records at any time. Just email maddie@elevatealign.com and we'll take care of it promptly.

Cookies and local storage

We use your browser's local storage to remember your email address so forms on our pages auto-fill for your convenience. This data never leaves your browser and is not accessible to us. You can clear it at any time through your browser settings.

Children's privacy

Our tools are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

If we make significant changes to this policy, we'll update the date at the top of this page.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at maddie@elevatealign.com — we're real people and we'll actually respond.
