Tips & tricks

01 — Batch it

Too many to do at once? Sort in batches.

Already-sorted photos are in subfolders and invisible to the tool. Next time you open the same folder, only unsorted files show up. Stop, extract more, come back — your progress never disappears.

02 — Go deeper

Broad first, specific second.

Sort everything into wide buckets fast. Then open any sub-folder and sort it further with new keys. Stock becomes: Landscapes, Portraits, Events. Every folder can be its own session.

03 — Sort for someone else

Works on any Mac or PC in Chrome.

Download the links from your purchase email and open the tool on anyone's computer. Great for helping a parent or family member sort their camera roll before saving to a hard drive.

04 — External drives

Sorts directly on any drive you plug in.

SD card, external hard drive, USB stick — files move on the drive itself. Sort straight onto a hard drive without copying anything to your main computer first.
