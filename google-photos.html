Google Photos Sorter

Sort your Google Photos into albums — one at a time, one keypress each. Works on any device, any browser.

One-time setup (5 min)

1Go to console.cloud.google.com → Create a new project (name it anything)
2Search for "Photos Library API" → Enable it
3Go to APIs & Services → Credentials → Create Credentials → OAuth Client ID
4Application type: Web application. Under "Authorized JavaScript origins" add: https://eauco-mads.github.io
5Copy your Client ID and paste it below

Your photos never leave your browser or Google's servers. This tool only adds photos to albums — it never deletes, moves, or modifies anything.
