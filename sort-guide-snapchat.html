Snapchat Edition

Sort Your Snap Memories
in 5 Minutes

Get everything straight to your computer, then sort with one keypress per photo

01

Step One

Get your photos onto your computer

From Snapchat, straight to your computer

1On your computer, go to accounts.snapchat.com and log in
2Click My Data, scroll to Memories, click Submit Request
3Snapchat emails you a download link, usually within a few hours
4Click the link, download the zip, unzip it. Your memories are ready

From iPhone Camera Roll

1Open Photos, tap Select in the top right corner
2Select the photos you want, then tap the share icon
3Tap Save to Files and choose a folder on your Mac
4Or AirDrop the photos to your Mac and drop them in a folder
Pro tip: Create one folder on your Desktop called Unsorted and dump everything there. Snap memories, iPhone photos, all of it. That is your starting point and zero prep is needed beyond that.
