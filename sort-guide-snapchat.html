Snapchat Edition

Sort Your Snap Memories
in 20 Minutes

Get everything straight to your computer — then sort with one keypress per photo

01

Step One

Get your photos onto your computer

From Snapchat → straight to computer

1On your computer, go to accounts.snapchat.com → log in
2Click My Data → scroll to Memories → Submit Request
3Snapchat emails a download link (usually within a few hours)
4Download the zip → unzip it → your memories are ready

From iPhone Camera Roll

1Open Photos → tap Select (top right)
2Select what you want → tap the share icon
3Tap Save to Files → pick a folder (via iCloud or cable)
4Or: AirDrop to your Mac and drop into a folder
Pro tip: Create one folder called Unsorted on your Desktop. Dump everything there — Snap memories, iPhone photos, all of it. That's your starting point.
