Snapchat Edition

Sort Your Snap Memories
One Keypress at a Time

Get everything straight to your computer, then sort with one keypress per photo

01

Step One

Get your Snap Memories onto your computer

Request your Memories from Snapchat

1On your computer, go to accounts.snapchat.com and log in
2Click My Data, scroll to Memories, click Submit Request
3Snapchat emails you a download link within a few hours — click it to download. You may receive multiple zip files (that is normal)
4Create a folder on your Desktop called Snap Memories. Extract each zip file (Mac: double-click it / Windows: right-click → Extract All), then drag all the .jpg and .mp4 files from the extracted folders into your Snap Memories folder
5Open Photo Sorter in Chrome and click Choose Folder — select your Snap Memories folder (not the zip file)

Also have iPhone photos to sort?

1Open the Photos app on your iPhone, tap Select
2Select what you want, tap the share icon, tap Save to Files
3Save to the same Unsorted folder as your Snap memories
4Or just AirDrop the photos to your Mac and drop them in the folder
Create one folder on your Desktop called Snap Memories and drop all your extracted photo and video files in there. Once you have chosen that folder in Photo Sorter, your sorted subfolders (Travel, Best, etc.) will appear inside that same Snap Memories folder as you sort. Open Finder → Desktop → Snap Memories to find them when you are done.
