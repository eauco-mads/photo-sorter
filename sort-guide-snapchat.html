👻 Snapchat Edition

Sort Your Snap Memories
in 20 Minutes

Export directly to your computer — no camera roll step needed

01

Step One

Download your Snap Memories directly to your computer

1

On your computer, go to accounts.snapchat.com and log in

2

Click My Data in the left menu

3

Scroll to Memories & Snaps → click Submit Request

4

Snapchat emails you a download link — usually arrives within a few hours

5

Click the link in the email → download the zip file → unzip it

6

Your photos and videos are now in a folder on your computer — ready to sort!

⏱ Heads up: The download request can take a few hours. Submit it now, sort other photos while you wait, then come back and knock out your Memories when the email arrives.
