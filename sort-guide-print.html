Sort Your Photos
in 20 Minutes

Export · Organize · Done — the system that actually sticks

01

Step One

Get your photos off your phone

From Snapchat → straight to your computer

1On your computer go to accounts.snapchat.com → log in
2Click My Data → scroll to Memories → click Submit Request
3Snapchat emails you a download link (usually within a few hours)
4Download the zip → unzip it → your memories are ready to sort. Done!

From iPhone Camera Roll

1Open Photos → tap Select (top right)
2Select the photos you want → tap share icon
3Tap Save to Files → pick a folder on your Mac (via iCloud or cable)
4Or: AirDrop photos to your Mac and drop them in a folder
Pro tip: Create one folder on your Desktop called Unsorted and dump everything there. That is your starting point — no other prep needed.
02

Step Two

Set up your four folders

T Travel Anywhere you went, day trips, adventures
P Personal Memories, family, friends, everyday life
S Stock Quality shots to sell, print, or post professionally
A Archive Not relevant now but not ready to delete
