01

Step One

Get your images into one folder on your computer

From SD Card / Camera

1Plug in SD card or connect camera via USB
2Open Finder → locate the SD card under Locations
3Navigate to DCIM folder → select all → copy to a folder on your Desktop
4Or drag directly from Image Capture app (built into Mac)

From iPhone / Existing Folders

1AirDrop a batch to your Mac, or plug in iPhone → open Image Capture
2Select all → import to a new folder
3For existing messy folders: just point the sorter at them directly
4Works with external hard drives too — no need to copy first
Pro tip: You don't need to copy files to sort them. Just point the tool at whatever folder or drive they're already on. Sort in place.
