01

Step One

Point the tool at wherever your photos already live

Already on your computer or hard drive?

1You do not need to move or copy anything first
2Open the tool in Chrome, click Choose Folder
3Navigate to wherever your photos already are — Desktop, external drive, SD card, anywhere
4Select that folder and start sorting. Files move on whatever drive you picked

Coming off a camera or iPhone?

1Plug in your camera or SD card via USB, or AirDrop from iPhone
2Open Image Capture (built into Mac) and import to a folder
3Or open Finder, find the SD card under Locations, and copy the DCIM folder to your Desktop
4Then point the sorter at that folder and go
The short version: The tool works on any folder, anywhere. External drive, SD card, Desktop, does not matter. No importing required if your files are already somewhere accessible.
