01

Step One

Get your images into one folder on your computer

From SD Card or Camera

1Plug in your SD card or connect your camera via USB
2Open Finder and locate the SD card under Locations
3Navigate to DCIM, select all, copy to a Desktop folder
4Or use Image Capture, built into Mac, to import directly

From iPhone or Existing Folders

1AirDrop a batch to your Mac, or plug in via cable
2Open Image Capture, select all, import to a new folder
3For existing folders: just point the sorter at them directly
4Works on external hard drives too, no need to copy first
Pro tip: You do not need to copy files before sorting. Point the tool at whatever folder or drive they are already on and sort in place.
