Quick start

1

Export your photos first

Snapchat: Settings → Memories → Export to Camera Roll. iPhone: Photos → Select → Save to Files. Dump into one folder called Unsorted.

2

Open the right tool for you

On a computer → use the Desktop Sorter in Chrome. On your phone or anywhere → use the Google Photos Sorter (sign in with Google, no setup needed).

3

One keypress per photo

T Travel P Personal S Stock A Archive D Duplicate → Skip U Undo
4

Done — for real

Files move instantly. Albums create automatically. When the queue is empty, you're finished. No extra steps.
