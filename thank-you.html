Quick start

1

Export your photos

Snapchat: Settings → Memories → Export to Camera Roll. iPhone: Photos → Select → Save to Files. Dump everything into one folder called Unsorted.

2

Open the tool in Chrome

Click "Open Tool" above. Click Choose Folder and select your Unsorted folder. Your first photo appears immediately.

3

Sort with one keypress each

T Travel P Personal S Stock A Archive D Duplicate → Skip U Undo
4

Done

Files move instantly on your computer. When the queue is empty, your photos are sorted. No extra steps.
